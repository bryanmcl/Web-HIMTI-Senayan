WHO WE ARE

HIMTI @Senayan started from the merging of BINARY and HIMTI in 2021. Binary (BINUS INTERNATIONAL Computer Society) was formed from a group of Computer Science students batch 2013 and established in 2010 as mean to strengthen the bond among CS Students.

Himpunanan Mahasiswa Teknik Informatika (HIMTI) is a student organisation at Bina Nusantara University in the form of Departmental Student Association (HMJ) for School of Computer Science (SOCS) students, BINUS UNIVERSITY.

HIMTI BINUS @Senayan aims to accommodate and support every aspiration and progress of the academic and non-academic sectors of the members of the Informatics Engineering Student Association of Bina Nusantara University @Senayan.

OUR VISION & MISSION

VISION

"Making HIMTI BINUS University a recognised student association in Southeast Asia by 2025."

MISSION

Strengthening the bond between fellow HIMTI family members with knowledge, experience, and good values in forming one solid HIMTI family.

Instilling and developing a professional attitude in every individual within HIMTI, in order to be ready to face the world of industry, careers, and the development of a global environment.
